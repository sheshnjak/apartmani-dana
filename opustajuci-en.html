Relaxing vacation

If you couldn't wait for the start of your vacation then you will surely need few days to clear up your head and refill the batteries. Turanj is an ideal destination for that - besides its quietness and stillness you can feel real Mediterranean spirit here. Hospitality and stress-unawareness are our trade marks. Islands around Turanj, and walk by it's 4 km of coast will help you forget all the stress and rush through the year. Here you will find out that you don't have to rush anywhere and that days are long enough for everything.

After you take first few days of sunning and relaxing we suggest that you explore nearby islands. After that, you will surely come back to Turanj next year. You can rent a traditional fishing boats (5 HP) or sport boats (75 HP) for those who want drive fast among attractive islands. If you are more natural type, you can rent a windsurfing board and enjoy symbiosis of two elements - wind and sea.

Besides that we suggest that you visit some of numerous national parks around us. Take at least one day to visit Kornati archipelago - one of natures true miracles.
