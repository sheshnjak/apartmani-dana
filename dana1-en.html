Apartments Dana I

House Dana I has four single room and two double room apartments. House is placed just by the sea, it's right across the street.

Each apartment has it's own separated balconie with a beautiful view towards the sea and islands in the channel. Apartments also have kitchens, bathrooms and reserved parking space. Air condition, satelite TV and free internet access are inclued in the prices. In case that our guests have their own boat we can arrange a berth for it.

Four single bedroom apartments 1/2+1

We have four single bedroom apartments that are suitable for two or three persons. One of these apartments is placed on the midfloor and it has separated entrance, and the rest are on the second floor.
