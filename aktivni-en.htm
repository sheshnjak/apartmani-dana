Active vacation

In case that you are not the kind that comes to vacation only to lay around, you will surely be interested in our additional offerings. For those who like it fast we have powerboats with 75 HP engines. And if you are more calm and "take it easy" type, you can rent a traditional fishing boats with 5 HP engine and take your familiy to one of many beautifull islands nearby. You can use these boats for fishing trips too.

Sea around Turanj is impecably celan and clear, so it's ideal for diving. It's at most 15 m deep, which is a good start for amaters and enthusiasts. Buying one of those cheap undewater cameras is a great idea. That way you will have nice undervater pictures to show off when you come back home.

If you want to really feel the sea, we advice you to try the best way to learn about sailing - windsurfing. We are renting all kinds of windsurfing boards, and if you are interested we can help you with your first steps in windsurfing. Currently we have 5 kids models, 6 beginners and 4 profi - boards.

Besides sea and islands there are lots of other interesting stuff in Turanj. You can rent a bike or a moped to go and explore surrounding area. There are some truly beautiful roads by the sea, and one great mountain road through hills over Turanj.

Of course, you wan't be restricted only to what we have to offer you. There are other offerings from other people. We have football and basketball playgrounds and waterpolo pool in Turanj, and it's free for anyone's use. And last, but not the least: you definitely have to visit national parks around Turanj, few of them are true natures miracles like Kornati islands and Paklenica canyons.
