How to find us

Turanj is placed on the shoreline between Biograd and Zadar, and it's easiest to come to us by car or bus. If you are coming over newly built highway, you can get off in Zadar or Biograd exit and you'll be in Turanj in less than 20 minutes.

Italian guests often come to Zadar by ferryboat from Ancona, which makes their trip quick and effortless. In case you are coming from bigger distances, you can come on a plain. There is an international airport in Zadar and Split, and it can be arranged that we come to pick you and your family at the airport.

Regardless of ways you are coming to us it is advisable that you contact us by phone. That way we can help you find the easiest way to travel, suggest which road to choose, or help you any other way.
