Apartments Dana II

House Dana II has two single bedroom and one double bedroom apartment. House is only 50 m distant from house Dana I, and is newly built. It is also like Dana I placed in first row of houses, just by the sea. Event though facade is not entirely finished, house interior is completely finished and equipped. Apartments have kitchen, bathroom and balcony. Air condition, satelite TV and free internet access are inclued in the prices. There is also parking place reserved for every guest.
