Pedisić family has a lots of experience in tourism business, and that is why you can expect top service and flexibility from us. Besides quality of service it is always possible to ask for something else, and we are going to try our best to provide you with what you want. We are trying for some time to provide our guests with much more than just a bed and spot on a beach. That's why we have all kinds of extra offerings for you. It often happens that our guests keep coming back to us after they once came and saw how it feels to be our guest.
