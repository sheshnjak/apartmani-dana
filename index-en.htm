Then come to the place where the time has stopped. To Turanj, center of east Adriatic coast. Mediterranean spirit of a small coast village will surely help you relax.

Turanj is situated on north side of Pašman channel, in the middle of archipelago very similar to famous Kornati, only smaller and closer to mainland. If you are staying in Turanj these beautiful islands are only few minutes away.

We are renting three stars apartments classified as Studio type. They are all placed in our two houses only twenty meters from the sea. All the apartments have balconies looking at these islands and Pašman channel. Besides apartments we are offering you some additional activities to make your vacation even more fun and enjoying. In Croatia there is an old saying that best things don't last long, and vacation in Turanj is definitely one of them. The fact that so many people keep coming back speaks for itself...
